1. Authentication via Google OAuth 2.0

AimDo uses Google OAuth 2.0 for user authentication. We do not collect, see, or store your Google account password. When you sign in, Google provides us with a secure, encrypted token that allows us to access limited profile information (Name, Email, and Profile Picture) to create and maintain your account. You can manage or revoke this access at any time through your Google Account security settings.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data: Name and profile image retrieved through Google Sign-In.
  • User Content: Tasks, goals, and records created within the app.
  • Transaction Data: Subscription status managed via Google Play Billing.

3. Data Storage (Supabase)

All user records and identity data are securely hosted on Supabase. We implement industry-standard security protocols and encryption to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may process technical identifiers (like AAID) to serve ads.
  • Google Play Store: For processing secure financial transactions. We do not store credit card details.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Users have full control over their data. You can delete individual records in the app. For a full account and data deletion request, please contact us at the email provided above.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be effective immediately upon publication on this page with a revised "Effective Date."
